The Mount of Mercy | ደብረ ምሕረት

“I praise you seven times a day” ፨ “ስብዐ ለዕለትየ እሴብሐከ”

Psalm 118:164 ፨ መዝሙር ዘዳዊት ፻፲፰፡፻፷፬

This web app is designed to aid Orthodox Christians and catachumens towards a life of unceasing prayer. It provides access to essential daily prayers in multiple languages, including Amharic, English, Ge'ez, Spanish, and Tigrinya with phonetic transliterations to assist in learning and pronunciation.

📱 Install App to Your Phone

For easy access and offline use, you can install this web app to your mobile home screen:

  • iOS (Safari): Tap the "Share" icon and then select "Add to Home Screen".
  • Android (Chrome): Tap the three-dots menu and select "Install App" or "Add to Home screen".

💻 Install App to Your Computer

For easy access and offline use, you can also install this web app on your desktop computer:

  • Chrome:
    • Look for the "Install" icon (a computer with a down arrow) in the address bar on the right side, and click "Install".
    • Alternatively, tap the three-dots menu in the top right, then click "Cast, Save, & Share" then "Install".
  • Safari (macOS): Go to "File" in the menu bar and select "Add to Dock".

📖 Translations

The English translations of the daily prayers in this app are a work-in-progress and not yet approved by clergy. This English translation is intended to be a more accurate improvement upon the few existing English resources. Several sources are actively being consulted, including:

  • ውዳሴ ማርያም Widasie Maryam | The Praises of St. Mary: In Ge’ez & English, 3rd Edition translated by Living Tewahdo Ministry in March 2024
  • Daily Prayers of the Ethiopian Orthodox Täwaḥədo Church, Revised Pocket Edition translated by Archdeacon Täsfa Mika’el Williams in May 2020
  • Book of Prayer - መጽሐፈ ጸሎት: Theotokia (Weddase Mariam) / Selected Psalms, Litanies and Prayers from the Ethiopic Book of the Hours, Missal, and other Liturgical Sources translated by Father Mebratu Kiros (PhD) in 2022
  • የየዘወትር ጸሎት (The Daily Prayer) published by ethiopianorthodox.org
  • መሠረተ ግእዝ የግእዝ ንባብ መማሪያ መጽሐፍ ክፍል ፩ በሦስት ቋንቋዎች (The Foundation of Gə'əz: Gə'əz Reading Textbook, 1st Edition - In 3 languages) translated by ሊቀ ጠበብት ተክሌ ሲራክ (Liqe Tebebt Tekle Sirak) in 2023
  • Libro de Oraciones (Book of Prayers) published by the Puerto Rico Debre Besrat Kidus Gebriel Iglesia Ortodoxa Tewahedo de Etiopía in 2025
